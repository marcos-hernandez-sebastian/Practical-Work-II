Computer Science

Computer Engineering is a degree aimed at training professionals capable of operating in scenarios related to problem-solving and its interpretation, and the design of structures through various programming languages.

The curriculum is organized into 4 academic years, structured into modules and classes that fundamentally combine subjects in mathematics, programming, and electronics.
